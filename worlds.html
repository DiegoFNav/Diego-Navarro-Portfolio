Worlds

An Interdimensional Journey

About Worlds

The Multiverse awaits

Worlds follows the adventure of a boy from an isolated island who dreams of exploring lands across the ocean. He finds himself caught up in a battle that spans the multiverse. Tasked with protecting dimensions under attack, he sets off to form a team to fight against the coming darkness.

Luna

The Isolated Island

In a small farming village on the coast of the island Luna, the player begins their journey. Luna's people have never seen anything off the island. Their world is completely covered in water with the island as the only form of civilization.

Joining up with their childhood friend Abbey, the player sets off on their usual routine. They constantly stare into the night sky hoping there would be a way to leave Luna.

Capital City

Center of the Multiverse

A society of robots named the Minibots discovered the power to travel across dimensions. For ages their world served as the center of the Multiverse as they explored vast dimensions. They rightly named their world Capital City.

However, the leaders of the Minibots named the High Council shut off interdimensional travel centuries ago. Now with evidence that someone else has achieved Multiverse travel, King Minibot heads out to investigate. Joining the player they discover a threat coming to the Multiverse.

Astertin

The World Divided

The world of Astertin is controlled by two civilizations of people, the Aquinosians and the Teratins. For ages they coexisted together, however when interdimensional connection was shut off they quickly seperated. The Aquinosians, merpeople with expertise in magic set off to the deep ocean. The Teratins, insectoids with advanced technology stayed in the lush forests of the land.

Now the player arrives seeking help only to find two groups unwilling to co-operate as they face invasion from the nefarious Dr. Konlo.
